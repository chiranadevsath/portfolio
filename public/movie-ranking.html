MY ALL TIME FAVOURITE TV-SERIES

TOP 3 ARE GIVEN BELOW

1. TILL THE END OF THE MOON

Showcases a dark romance of the devil god and a immortal girl who goes back into the past to stop him from becoming evil. Its a mainland C-Drama and the plot and premise so cool and intriguing to watch. Currently my all time favourite.

2. BROOKLYN NINE-NINE

NINE NINE. That should sum up why I love this TV-series

3. VINCENZO

Koren Mafia TV series. Its also a dark vibe between a Italian Mafia and a lawyer. Really interesting story although the plot sometimes was all over the place it was my introduction to asian TV series so I enjoyed that show very much.
